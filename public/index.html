Steven Errington

Postdoctoral Research Associate | Neuroscience | Monosov Lab

I am a postdoctoral fellow in the Monosov Lab at Washington University School of Medicine, studying the cortical and subcortical systems that control exploration and learning. I combine behavioral studies in non-human primates with single-unit recordings, high-channel electrophysiology, and pharmacology. Before moving to WUSTL, I was a PhD-student in the Schall lab at Vanderbilt University, studying how the medial frontal cortex contributes to cognitive control.
